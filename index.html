About Me

Hi, I’m Antonio Sotelo, but you can call me Tony. Born and raised in Los Angeles, I bring over six years of experience in the entertainment industry, specializing in 3D, visual effects, and motion graphics. My passion lies in crafting visuals that stop the scroll and captivate audiences—from seamless CGI integration to dynamic, attention-grabbing social media ad designs.

I’ve had the honor of contributing to projects with Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, Disney, MGM, and many others, blending creativity and technical expertise to enhance the magic of cinema and marketing.

When I’m not creating, you’ll find me spending quality time with my wife and kids, diving into new skills, or sneaking in a workout when life allows. Let’s create something extraordinary together.
